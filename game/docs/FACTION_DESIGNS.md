# THE DEEP STATE: Master Design Specification (v3)

**The Narrative Core:** The Kirk Catalyst. Charlie Kirk has been "Redacted" because he possessed the **Epstein File**—evidence of the Regency King's most heinous crimes. The world is in a state of "Information Containment."

---

## 🌍 GLOBAL MECHANIC: THE WITNESS WAR
Every civilian on the map is a **Tug-of-War Resource**. They start in a "Terrified" state (Panic flee). Factions fight to shift their state:

| State | Faction Goal | Result |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Detained** | **Regency** | Silenced. Moved to Compliance Pens for §Administrative Fees. |
| **Empowered** | **Frontline** | Stationary/Broadcasting. Generates massive §Viral Clout. |
| **Radicalized** | **Sovereign** | Converted into a basic combat unit (Militia). |
| **Assetized** | **Oligarchy** | Held for blackmail/leverage. Provides §Insurance/Bounty bonuses. |

**The MacGuffin:** **The Tape (USB Data Core).** Dropped at the site of Kirk's death. Holding it provides a massive buff to the faction's primary economic resource.

---

## 🏛️ 1. THE REGENCY
**Thematic Core:** "The Official Version." Bureaucratic complicity and patriotic brutalism.
**Styling:** Navy Blue / Gold / Cyan. All buildings have a **Red Hat** roof. Tickers display `NARRATIVE STABILIZED`.
**Economy:** **Incarceration Credits.** Income per Detained civilian.

### 🪖 Unit Roster
- `Gravy Seal`: Standard Infantry. Tactical mall-gear.
- `ICE Agent`: Documentation Specialist. Attacks slow enemies ("Checking Papers").
- `Patriot Lawyer`: Support. Throws "Cease & Desist" files (Area Suppression).
- `Proud Perimeter`: Elite Melee. "Stand Back & Stand By" (Protection Aura).
- `The Q-Anon`: Signal Jammer. "Alternative Facts" aura resets "Witness Leak" bars.
- `The Donor`: Passive Buff. Man in golf shirt. 2x Infamy penalty for attacker if killed.
- `Compliance Bus`: High-cap transport (30 seats). Crucial for Witness Detention.
- `MRAP`: Heavy Siege. Armored "Social Stabilizer."

### 🏗️ Structures
- **Administrative HQ (Palace):** Required for "Authorization" (Building).
- **Compliance Barracks:** Infantry production.
- **Motorpool Depot:** Vehicle production.
- **Compliance Pen:** Economy. Where Detained witnesses are "Processed."
- **Patriot Training Center:** Elite Tech. Unlocks "The Gavel Drop" global ability.

---

## 📡 2. THE FRONTLINE
**Thematic Core:** "The Viral Narrative." Accidental heroes fighting for Subscribers.
**Styling:** Neon Green / Black / Glitch effects. Solar panels and satellite dishes.
**Economy:** **Viral Clout.** Income scales with the number of "Empowered" witnesses in sight.

### 🪖 Unit Roster
- `Digital Nomad`: Standard. Generates §Credits while in combat.
- `FPV Operator`: Artillery. Launches one-use drone projectiles.
- `Agitator`: Support. Reduces enemy "Bureaucracy" (Morale) via Megaphone.
- `The Journalist`: Camera Unit. Non-combat. "Records" Regency atrocities to generate §Credits.
- `Influencer`: Passive. Increases rank-up speed for nearby units.
- `News Van`: Mobile Vision. Necessary to "Empower" witnesses via satellite link.

### 🏗️ Structures
- **Press Bureau HQ:** Command center.
- **Content House:** Infantry production.
- **ISR Relay:** Vehicle production.
- **Viral Antenna:** Economy. Boosts Clout generation in a large radius.

---

## 💜 3. THE SOVEREIGN UNION
**Thematic Core:** "Analog Resistance." Off-the-grid cells abused by the Gilded.
**Styling:** Dark Purple / Shadow / Camo Netting. Makeshift and industrial.
**Economy:** **Analog Zeal.** Resources gained by destroying Regency/Oligarchy infrastructure.

### 🪖 Unit Roster
- `Proxy`: Standard Guerrilla. Fast, glass-cannon.
- `Sleeper Agent`: Elite Stealth. Invisible until attacking.
- `The Prepper`: Trap Specialist. Lays "EMP Traps" that disable enemy HUD/Minimap.
- `Militia`: Radicalized Civilians. Expendable mass.
- `Technical`: Fast improvised gun-truck.
- `VBIED`: Suicide car. Massive shock value/Infamy.
- `Tunnel Borer`: Transport. Creates manhole exits anywhere in Fog.

### 🏗️ Structures
- **Safehouse (Captured):** Sovereign does not build; they **Squat** in captured buildings.
- **Hidden Tunnel:** Infantry spawn point (Looks like a bush or manhole).
- **Wreckage Garage:** Vehicle production (Built inside a pile of junk).
- **The Grotto:** Elite tech lab hidden underground.

---

## 💰 4. THE OLIGARCHY
**Thematic Core:** "The Human Resource." War profiteers and insurance scammers.
**Styling:** Black / Gold / Glass. Sleek corporate brutalism.
**Economy:** **Insurance Payouts.** Passive §Credits whenever a friendly unit is killed.

### 🪖 Unit Roster
- `Conscript`: Cheap, empathy-free meat shield.
- `Contractor`: Mercenary. "Invoices" enemy buildings (Siphons credits).
- `Troll Unit`: Disruption. Erodes enemy capture progress from range.
- `Wagner Unit`: Elite Shock Troop. High-output kinetic platform.
- `The CEO`: Objective Master. "Issues Contracts" (Timed missions for bonus §).
- `Salvage Truck`: Vehicle. Recovers § from wrecks (Scrubbing the scene).
- `Heavy Hauler`: Heavy Tank. Can crush civilian vehicles to clear paths.

### 🏗️ Structures
- **Executive Boardroom:** Command center.
- **Staffing Agency:** Infantry production.
- **Salvage Yard:** Vehicle production and wreck processing.
- **Insurance Office:** Economy. Required for the "Payout" logic.
