# THE DEEP STATE: System Deep Dive & Satirical Nuance

This document expands on the core mechanics to ensure the satirical tone (Post-Truth, Bureaucracy, Complicity) is baked into the gameplay.

---

## 📉 1. THE MORALE SYSTEM: "QUIET QUITTING"
Instead of standard "Morale," units have a **Bureaucracy Meter**.
- **The Mechanic:** As friendly units die or the "Epstein File" is leaked, Bureaucracy drops.
- **The State: "Quiet Quitting":** When Bureaucracy hits 0, units enter this state for 30s.
    - They ignore move/attack orders.
    - They play a unique animation: "Checking Phone" or "Filling out TPS Reports."
    - They can be "Re-Engaged" by a `Donor` (Regency), `CEO` (Oligarchy), or `Influencer` (Frontline) standing near them.
- **Satire:** The units aren't brave; they are just waiting for their shift to end.

---

## 📦 2. THE LOGISTICS SYSTEM: "RED TAPE & SUPPLIES"
Units have limited **Supplies** (Ammo/Documentation).
- **Regency:** Units use "Documentation Forms." When empty, they cannot "Audit" (Attack). They must return to a `Compliance Bus` or `Administrative HQ` to "Restock."
- **Sovereign:** Units use "Zeal." They don't restock; they "Pray/Meditate" at a `Safehouse` to refill.
- **Frontline:** Units use "Battery Life." They must return to a `News Van` or `Solar Array` to recharge.
- **Oligarchy:** Units use "Expense Accounts." They restock by being near a `Salvage Truck`.

---

## 👁️ 3. VISUAL REDACTION (FOG OF WAR)
Fog of War isn't just "Black." It reflects the faction's worldview:
- **Regency:** **Classified Fog.** The unexplored map is covered in horizontal black censor bars.
- **Frontline:** **Low-Bitrate Fog.** Unexplored areas are extreme, blocky pixels. "Buffered" areas (recently seen) are slightly clearer.
- **Sovereign:** **The Analog Void.** The map is a dark, hand-drawn topographical sketch. No neon grid.
- **Oligarchy:** **Un-Monetized Fog.** Unexplored areas have "Buy This Sector" or "Pending Valuation" watermarks.

---

## 🗣️ 4. THE WITNESS TUG-OF-WAR (DEEP DIVE)
Civilians are the "Ball" in this game. They have two competing bars: **STRESS** and **LEAK.**

### The Leak Bar (Frontline/Sovereign Tool)
- Increases when a `Journalist` (Frontline) records them or a `Prepper` (Sovereign) talks to them.
- If it hits 100%, the Witness "Blows the Whistle." This creates a permanent **Narrative Scar** on the map (a zone where the Regency cannot generate income).

### The Stress Bar (Regency/Oligarchy Tool)
- Increases when an `ICE Agent` "Checks Papers" or an `MRAP` fires nearby.
- If it hits 100%, the Witness "Complies." They become a passive asset that can be herded into a `Compliance Bus`.

### The Q-Anon "Debunking" Aura
- The `Q-Anon` unit acts as a **Leak-Resetter**. Standing near a witness with a high "Leak Bar" triggers the "Alternative Facts" ability, which drains the bar rapidly. 
- **Satire:** "I'm just asking questions!"

---

## 📁 5. THE MACGUFFIN: "THE TAPE" (USB DRIVE)
- **Carrier Effects:** The unit carrying the Tape moves 20% slower.
- **Regency Victory:** If the Regency carries the Tape to the `Administrative HQ` and holds it for 2 minutes, they "Redact" it. **Mission Win (Official Version).**
- **Frontline Victory:** If they carry it to a `Viral Antenna` and hold it for 2 minutes, they "Upload" it. **Mission Win (The Leak).**
- **Oligarchy Goal:** They don't want to win; they want to **Hold** it. Every 10s they hold the Tape, they "Invoice" the Regency for §500.

---

## 👑 6. THE KING (THE NARRATIVE BOSS)
The "King of the Regency" is never seen as a unit. He is a **UI Element**.
- He occasionally sends **"Directives"** (Pop-ups) that force the player to change ROE or target specific witnesses.
- **Satire:** The player is the "Middle Manager" protecting a monster they’ve never met.
- **Final Mission:** The "King's Palace" map, where the other three factions are all sieging the center simultaneously.

---

## 🚧 7. CONSTRUCTION NUANCES
- **Regency "Authorization":** You place a building ghost. It sits there at 0% HP. You must send an `ICE Agent` to "Notarize" it before the build progress starts.
- **Sovereign "Squatting":** They don't have a build menu. They have a "Capture Menu." Capturing a neutral `Campus Cafe` gives them the choice: "Safehouse" or "Ammo Cache."
- **Frontline "Streaming":** Buildings are "Dropped" from the sky. They are fast but fragile.
- **Oligarchy "Sub-Contracting":** You pay double § to have the building appear instantly, but it arrives with 50% HP.
