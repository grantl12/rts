# THE DEEP STATE: Theater Storyboards

The "Mirror-Map" strategy uses the same physical geometry for two different missions, changing the time of day, objectives, and "Narrative Layer" to maximize asset ROI and satirical depth.

---

## 🎭 Theater 1: The Quad of Compliance (The Incident Site)
**The Geometry:** A university plaza with a central memorial statue, an admin block, and a parking garage.

### ✊ Campaign: THE REGENCY (The Audit)
- **Vibe:** Daytime, bright, sterile.
- **Objective:** "Maintain Public Decorum." Capture the Memorial to stabilize the "Official Version" of the incident.
- **The Twist:** You have to protect the "Martyr's Statue" while it is being built. If the construction is interrupted, the "Narrative" glitches.

### ✊ Campaign: FRONTLINE (The Footage Leak)
- **Vibe:** Sunset, long shadows, glitchy UI.
- **Objective:** "Expose the Bronze." Reclaim the site to upload the raw, unedited footage of the Martyr's death.
- **The Twist:** The statue you protected in the previous campaign is now a "Suppression Tower" you must dismantle.

---

## 🎭 Theater 2: The Border Wall of Bureaucracy
**The Geometry:** A massive, unfinished concrete barrier that is actually a series of interconnected office cubicles and "Audit Stations" rather than a physical wall.

### ✊ Campaign: THE REGENCY (The Perimeter Check)
- **Objective:** "Index the Incoming." Process waves of "Digital Nomads" through the Audit Stations. 
- **Satire:** You aren't stopping people; you're just making sure they have the correct "Meta-Data" to exist in the country.

### ✊ Campaign: SOVEREIGN UNION (The Analog Breach)
- **Objective:** "The Great Unplugging." Use traditionalist units to physically destroy the Audit Stations and create an "Analog Zone" where the Neon Grid cannot reach.
- **Satire:** Destroying a wall of cubicles to "Free the Soul."

---

## 🎭 Theater 3: The Silicon Valley Sub-Ziggurat
**The Geometry:** A subterranean server farm disguised as a luxury "Digital Wellness" retreat.

### ✊ Campaign: THE OLIGARCHY (The Crypto-Cooling)
- **Objective:** "Liquidate the Assets." Secure the cooling vents to keep the PMC crypto-mining racks from overheating.
- **Satire:** Protecting "The Economy" while the surface world is in "Total Panic."

### ✊ Campaign: FRONTLINE (The Server Migration)
- **Objective:** "Crowdfund the Cloud." Infiltrate the Ziggurat and physically carry "Data Cores" to the surface to leak them.
- **Satire:** The units are literally carrying the "Shadow Powers" on their backs.

---

## 🎭 Theater 4: The Gated Community of Suspicion
**The Geometry:** A suburban cul-de-sac where every house has a surveillance drone and a "Property Value" aura.

### ✊ Campaign: THE REGENCY (The Neighborhood Audit)
- **Objective:** "Maintain Property Value." Use Park Rangers to "De-Zone" any house that has a "Frontline" flag.
- **Satire:** The RTS equivalent of a homeowners' association dispute, but with tactical scooters.

### ✊ Campaign: THE OLIGARCHY (The Kickback Collection)
- **Objective:** "Siphon the Equity." Use PMC Contractors to "Collect Dues" from the houses, siphoning funds into the Global Conflict DB.
- **Satire:** Collecting "Protection Money" disguised as a service fee.
