## 🥩 The Oligarchy's "Meat Grinder" Logic
The "Insurance Payout" combined with the conscription loop creates a dark, "win-win" scenario for the Contractor General:
 * **The Loop:** You load civilians into a **Labor Hub** -> they are converted into **"Contractor Conscripts"** -> you throw them at the enemy.
 * **The Payout:** If they survive, they take the point. If they die, you get a **Life Insurance Reimbursement** from the home office.
 * **The Satire:** The units have no names, only **Asset IDs**.

## 🤳 The Frontline's "Likes & Subscribes" Economy
The **Digital Nomads** don't want to "hold" civilians—they want them **watching**.
 * **The Crowd Effect:** The more civilians in a 50m radius of a combat zone, the higher the **Viral Multiplier**.
 * **The Goal:** Protect the civilians so they keep their phones out. If they flee or get "Audited" by the Regency, your income drops instantly.
 * **The Superpower:** **"Going Viral."** A temporary 200% income boost triggered when a civilian records a Regency "Execution Squad" in action.

## 🛠️ Backlog Update: Faction-Specific Population Logic

 * **Result:** Permanent income per head until the building is breached.

 * **Result:** Converts civilian units into low-tier "Meat" squads. Triggers payout on death.
### **Sovereign Logic (The Underground)**

 * **Result:** Converts civilians into specialized "Proxy" units or high-value "Sleeper Agents."
### **Frontline Logic (The Influencer)**

 * **Result:** Income scales with the number of "Active Observers" near your units.
