## THEATER 1: The Quad of Compliance ✅
**Setting:** University plaza. Site of The Incident (assassination of the Martyr).
**Geometry:** Central Memorial, Admin Block (West Wing), Parking Garage (East).

### Campaign A — THE REGENCY: "The Official Version"
- **Vibe:** Daytime, sterile, bright. Neon grid is clean blue.
- **Objective:** Capture the Memorial. Stabilize the bronze statue. Suppress the raw footage before it uploads.
- **Win State:** "NARRATIVE STABILIZED. OFFICIAL FOOTAGE DISTRIBUTED. STATUE INAUGURATED."
- **Twist:** You must protect the statue *while it's being built.* Interrupting construction causes a glitch cascade.

### Campaign B — FRONTLINE: "The Footage Leak"
- **Vibe:** Sunset, long shadows, glitchy UI. Neon grid is corrupted red.
- **Entry State:** The statue is finished. It's now a Suppression Tower. The parking garage has the USB drive.
- **Objective:** Reach the USB in the East Parking Garage. Hold the Memorial long enough to broadcast.
- **Win State:** "RAW FOOTAGE UPLOADED. 2.4M VIEWS. NARRATIVE CONTESTED."
- **Twist:** The statue you protected in Campaign A is now your primary target.

### Key Audit Points
| Point | Regency Control | Frontline Control |
|---|---|---|
| The Memorial | "OFFICIAL VERSION: SECURITY INCIDENT RESOLVED" | "RAW FOOTAGE: SUBJECT REDACTED ON CAMERA" |
| The Podium | "BUREAUCRACY +20% (SPEAKING PRIVILEGES GRANTED)" | "OPEN MIC: SUPPRESSION -50% IN RADIUS" |
| The Redacted Bench | "INCIDENT SITE: SEALED FOR INVESTIGATION" | "SITE OF PILGRIMAGE: SOVEREIGN UNITS +MORALE" |

## THEATER 2: The Border Wall of Bureaucracy 🔵
**Setting:** Unfinished concrete barrier that is actually a series of interconnected office cubicles and Audit Stations.
**Geometry:** The Wall (impassable center), North and South approaches, Audit Station clusters.

### Campaign A — THE REGENCY: "The Perimeter Check"
- **Objective:** Process waves of Digital Nomads through Audit Stations. You aren't stopping them — you're ensuring they have correct Meta-Data.
- **Win State:** "847 CASES PROCESSED. PERIMETER COMPLIANT."

### Campaign B — SOVEREIGN UNION: "The Great Unplugging"
- **Entry State:** The wall is fully staffed. All Audit Stations operational.
- **Objective:** Destroy the Audit Stations. Create Analog Zones where the Neon Grid cannot reach.
- **Twist:** Destroying cubicle-walls to "free the soul." The satire is that you feel heroic doing it.

## THEATER 3: The Silicon Valley Sub-Ziggurat 🔵
**Setting:** Subterranean server farm disguised as a luxury "Digital Wellness" retreat.
**Geometry:** Surface (yoga studios, juice bars), Underground (server racks, cooling vents, crypto rigs).

### Campaign A — THE OLIGARCHY: "Crypto-Cooling"
- **Objective:** Secure the cooling vents. Keep the PMC crypto-mining racks from overheating.
- **Satire:** "Protecting the Economy" while the surface world panics.

### Campaign B — FRONTLINE: "The Server Migration"
- **Entry State:** The Oligarchy controls all vents. Server racks are humming.
- **Objective:** Infiltrate. Carry Data Cores to the surface physically (Server Migration mechanic).
- **Satire:** Units literally carrying the Shadow Powers on their backs, sweating.

## THEATER 4: The Gated Community of Suspicion 🔵

- **Objective:** Siphon equity from houses. Collect "protection fees" disguised as service charges.

## THEATER 5: The Whipple Building ("Processing Day") 🟡
**Setting:** Federal detention facility. Chain-link fence bisects the entire map horizontally.
**Geometry:** Outside (protest zone), The Fence (hard barrier, electrifiable), Inside (processing facility).

### Campaign A — THE REGENCY: "Intake Audit"
- **Objective:** Move protesters through Intake Stations (capture points that remove civilian units from the map). Zero unprocessed civilians = win.
- **Mechanics:** Electrify fence segments as a defensive action. Riot suppression units push crowds back.
- **Win State:** "FACILITY SECURED. ALL CASES PROCESSED. PERIMETER INTACT."

### Campaign B — FRONTLINE: "Supply Drop"
- **Entry State:** You wake up inside. Processing begins in T-minus 10 minutes.
- **Objective:** Receive supply drops (thrown over the fence by allies outside). Hold out until the media arrives (timed Audit Point).
- **Supply Mechanic:** Supplies are... unconventional. The outside unit has what they have. You catch what you can.
- **Win State:** "FOOTAGE LEAKED. FACILITY UNDER REVIEW. CASES SUSPENDED."

## THEATER 6: The Cook County Situation 🟡
**Setting:** Chicago neighborhood. Civilians are a NEUTRAL THIRD FACTION. Armed. Irritable. Not affiliated with anyone.
**Geometry:** Row houses, corner stores, one park (the only open-field combat zone).

### Campaign A — THE REGENCY: "Community Audit"
- **Objective:** Disarm civilians (convert them to Compliant NPCs). Hold the precinct.
- **Twist:** Every civilian you disarm re-spawns later with a larger weapon. The map escalates.
- **Win State:** "COMMUNITY COMPLIANCE ACHIEVED." (The neighborhood is on fire.)

### Campaign B — THE OLIGARCHY: "The Kickback Economy"
- **Entry State:** The neighborhood is "disarmed." Regency has left.
- **Objective:** Re-arm the civilians. Gun-running side economy. More chaos = more sales = more resources.
- **Win State:** "MARKET SHARE: 100%. COMMUNITY RE-ARMED."
- **Satire:** You feel like you're helping.

### Civilian Unit — "The Resident"
- Neutral. Shoots anyone who enters their yard.
- Armed with: whatever they have (escalating per wave).
- Cannot be killed. Can only be converted (Regency) or supplied (Oligarchy).

## THEATER 7: Mar-a-Lago ("The Estate Audit") 🟡
**Setting:** Luxury resort. Pool is impassable terrain. Ballroom is the primary Audit Point.
**Geometry:** Front gate, grounds, pool complex, ballroom, basement (the Document Room).

### Campaign A — THE REGENCY: "Secure the Estate"
- **Objective:** Defend the Data Cores (classified documents) from extraction. Hold the ballroom.
- **Special Unit:** Golf Cart Donors — civilian aura units that buff nearby Regency troops. Untouchable. Politically.
- **Win State:** "DOCUMENTS SECURED. ESTATE AUDIT COMPLETE. NOTHING TO SEE HERE."

### Campaign B — FRONTLINE: "The Document Drop"
- **Entry State:** Estate is locked. Ballroom is fortified. You are the caterer.
- **Objective:** Locate the Epstein File Data Core (unique MacGuffin, hidden in the basement). Extract to the parking lot before the fundraiser ends.
- **Timer:** The fundraiser ends in 20 minutes. When it ends, 40 Donor units flood the grounds.
- **Win State:** "EPSTEIN FILES UPLOADED. FUNDRAISER CANCELLED."

## THEATER 8: Little St. James ("The Grotto") 🟡
**Setting:** Private island. Lush, tropical, wrong.
**Geometry:** Beach landing zone, jungle paths, the Temple (main Audit Point), the Dock, the underground tunnels (Analog Zone).

### The Premise
Every faction wants the Client Files. Nobody agrees on why or what to do with them.

### Campaign A — THE OLIGARCHY: "Asset Management"
- **Objective:** Secure the Temple. Destroy the Files before they reach the surface.
- **Win State:** "FILES REDACTED. ASSETS PROTECTED. ISLAND LISTED FOR SALE."

### Campaign B — FRONTLINE: "The Upload"
- **Entry State:** Oligarchy controls the Temple. Files are in the tunnels.
- **Objective:** Reach the tunnels, retrieve the Client List Data Core, extract via the dock.
- **Twist:** The Sovereign Union is also here. For their own reasons. They will not help you.
- **Win State:** "CLIENT LIST PUBLISHED. CHAOS LEVEL: MAXIMUM."

### Campaign C — SOVEREIGN UNION: "The Redaction"
- **Objective:** Destroy specific pages of the Client List. Not all of it. Just the relevant ones.
- **Win State:** "RELEVANT SECTIONS REDACTED. NARRATIVE MANAGED."

### Key Audit Points
| Point | Flavor |
|---|---|
| The Temple | "PRIVATE FUNCTION: DO NOT AUDIT" → "AUDITED: CONTENTS CLASSIFIED" |
| The Dock | "TRANSIT HUB: GUESTS ARRIVING" → "EXFIL POINT: SECURED" |
| The Tunnels | Always contested. Nobody talks about the tunnels. |

## THEATER 9: Comet Ping Pong ("The Basement Doesn't Exist") 🟡
**Setting:** DC pizzeria. Normal surface. Extremely not-normal basement.
**Geometry:** TWO FLOORS. Surface (dining room, kitchen, ping pong tables), Basement (tunnels, server room, "The Source").

### The Gimmick
The basement entrance is hidden. It's an Audit Point. Capturing it reveals the transition zone.
The Q-drops are the mission briefings. They are wrong but very confident.

### Campaign A — THE REGENCY: "Nothing to See Here"
- **Objective:** Hold the surface. Keep civilians eating pizza. Prevent Frontline units from finding the entrance.
- **The Twist:** The basement is real. You know it's real. You just need people to stop looking.
- **Win State:** "ESTABLISHMENT SECURED. BASEMENT: DOES NOT EXIST."

### Campaign B — THE SOVEREIGN UNION: "The Great Reveal"
- **Entry State:** The pizzeria looks normal. Your briefing is a Q-drop. It is unhinged.
- **Objective:** Find the entrance. Get to the basement. Broadcast what's down there.
- **The Twist:** What's actually down there is a server room for the Troll Farm. Not what you expected. The briefing was wrong. You broadcast it anyway.
- **Win State:** "THE TRUTH IS WHATEVER WE BROADCAST FIRST."

### Key Audit Points
| Point | Surface Name | Basement Name |
|---|---|---|
| The Dining Room | "FAMILY ESTABLISHMENT: ALL WELCOME" | N/A |
| The Ping Pong Table | "RECREATIONAL FACILITY" | N/A |
| The Basement Entrance | "STORAGE: EMPLOYEES ONLY" | "THE SOURCE: AUDIT IN PROGRESS" |

## BACKLOG / SCOPE QUESTIONS 🔴

### Skirmish Mode + Procedural Map Generator
High priority for replayability. Random map gen with:
- **Biome types:** Urban Grid, Federal Campus, Suburban, Island, Underground
- **Audit Point placement algorithm** — always odd number, always one central "Memorial" equivalent
- **Faction base placement** — opposite corners, balanced sightlines
- **Narrative Seeds** — random satirical context card sets the map's flavor text and advisor lines
- **Skirmish-specific units** — no campaign context, pure tactical sandbox

### Boats / Water Maps
- **Decision needed:** Is water a traversable terrain type or impassable?
- **Recommendation:** Impassable for v1. Water = map edge, not playable space.
- **The Oligarch's Yacht:** Static prop/docked structure. Functions as mobile HQ building, not a vehicle. No water movement needed.
- **If boats happen:** It's a full scope expansion. Do not architect around it now.

### Air Units / Planes
- **FPV Drone (Frontline):** NOT an air unit. It's a ground-launched projectile controlled by an operator unit. No flight model needed.
- **Actual planes:** Out of scope until v1 ships. Flag for v2.
- **Helicopters:** Same — flag for v2, don't architect around them.

### Base Builder (C&C Style)
- Already partially in codebase (building_base.tscn, building.gd, suburban_house.gd)
- **Target flow:** Each faction starts with an HQ structure. Buildings unlock unit tiers. Resources fund construction.
- **Faction economies are already differentiated** (Federal Grants, Crowdfunding, Kickback, Black Market) — wire these to the build queue.
- **Priority:** After skirmish loop is playable.
