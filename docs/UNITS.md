# Unit Design: The First Wave (Meat vs. Scalpel)

## 🏛️ General Mechanics
- **The Core Soul:** Every squad has one "Leader" sprite. If the leader dies, the squad loses its "Soul" (no veterancy gained, cannot be promoted to Hall of Heroes).
- **FTL-Style Systems:** Squads don't just have HP. They have:
    - **Vitality:** Physical health.
    - **Bureaucracy (Morale):** If this hits zero, the unit "Self-Audits" (flees the map).
    - **Supplies:** Ammo/Energy. When empty, they can only use melee/basic attacks.

---

## ✊ Faction: The Regency
### Unit: The "Park Ranger" (The Scalpel)
*Inspired by: C&C Sniper / FTL Zoltan (Efficiency)*
- **Vibe:** Over-equipped, tactical, "I'm just doing my job."
- **Deep System (FTL Inspo):** **"Thermal Scope"** - High accuracy, but drains **Supplies** quickly.
- **Soul Trait:** *Professionalism* - Increases Bureaucracy of nearby friendly units.
- **Weapon:** Precision "Non-Lethal" (but actually lethal) Bolt Action.

---

## ✊ Faction: The Oligarchy
### Unit: The "Conscript Mob" (The Meat)
*Inspired by: C&C Conscripts / FTL Mantis (Swarm)*
- **Vibe:** 10 guys in tracksuits with rusty AKs.
- **Deep System (FTL Inspo):** **"The Meat Grinder"** - When a member of the mob dies, the survivors gain a temporary "Adrenaline" buff to Bureaucracy.
- **Soul Trait:** *Cynicism* - Immune to "Fact Check" superweapons.
- **Weapon:** "Recycled" Automatic Rifles (High spread, low damage).

---

## 🛠️ Progression (The Hall of Heroes)
When a **Park Ranger** hits the Map Cap:
- **Promotion:** Can become a "Chief Auditor."
- **Global Buff:** Increases Federal Grant income by 2%.
- **Legacy Gear:** Unlocks "Tactical Segway" (Permanent Speed Boost).
