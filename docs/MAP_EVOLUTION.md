# 🗺️ ARCHITECTURAL BRIEF: THE EVOLVING WORLD
**Project:** *The Deep State* **Core Philosophy:** "Shared-World Evolution" (Map Reuse & Persistence)

## 🏗️ 1. THE MAP EVOLUTION SYSTEM (Visual Scarring)
Each map is visited 2–3 times throughout the campaign. Instead of resetting, the environment tracks your **Infamy** and **ROE** across sessions.

### **Phase 1: The Incident (Pristine)**
 * **Visuals:** Green grass, functional infrastructure, high civilian traffic.
 * **Atmosphere:** Sunny, "Administrative" vibes.
 * **Gameplay:** Focus on "Auditing" and stealthy extractions.

### **Phase 2: The Occupation (Scarred)**
 * **Visuals:** Concrete barricades, scorched earth, persistent vehicle wrecks.
 * **Atmosphere:** Overcast, floodlights, "Managed Chaos."
 * **Gameplay:** High-intensity riot suppression and "Compliance Pen" defense.

### **Phase 3: The Redaction (Shattered)**
 * **Visuals:** Ruined buildings, massive black "Censor" tarps over landmarks.
 * **Atmosphere:** Night-time, glitching UI, heavy "Redaction Fog."
 * **Gameplay:** Survival/Bunker defense with 60% of the map blacked out by "Classified" zones.

## 📍 2. THE "TRIPLE THREAT" MISSION ARCHITECTURE
Every deployment has three distinct layers of objectives to maximize replayability.

| Layer | Type | Objective Example | Reward |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Primary** | **Directive** | "Secure the Campus Server Farm." | Unlocks next map/story progression. |
| **Secondary A** | **The Harvest** | "Extract 20 High-Value Assets (Civs)." | Primary source of **Administrative Credits**. |
| **Secondary B** | **The Satire** | "Redact the Student Newspaper Archive." | Buffs **Public Trust** or grants Hero Units. |

## 🩸 3. RESIDUE & TRAUMA LOGIC
The map physically remembers where you messed up.
 * **Mass Casualty Zones:** Sites where ROE 5 was used are marked with **Dried Blood** and **Discarded Caution Tape** on subsequent visits.
 * **Site Auras:**
   * **Trauma Zone:** Enemy units gain a "Vengeance" buff; Regency units get a "Guilt" debuff.
   * **Compliance Zone:** Regency units heal faster; Civilians are easier to tether.
 * **Navigation Decay:** Persistent debris (burned buses, rubble) permanently alters the AI's pathfinding, turning simple roads into tactical "Kill Boxes" by Visit 3.

## 🚗 4. VEHICLE PERSISTENCE & IPAWS INTEGRATION
 * **The Scar Tissue:** Destroyed civilian cars do not vanish; they become permanent cover or "IED Traps" in later missions.
 * **Navigation Server:** Persistent debris physically alters pathfinding maps between mission phases.

## 🎤 5. THE POST-OP LOOP (META-GAME)
After every visit, the player must face:
 1. **The Allocation Screen:** Assigning harvested civilians to "Silos" (Labor, PR, or Research).
 2. **The Press Briefing:** Choosing a "Spin" (Gaslight, Double Down, or Redact) to manage the Infamy generated during the mission.
