# 📑 BACKLOG ADDENDUM: THE "POPULATION HARVEST" SYSTEM
**Project:** *The Deep State* **Feature Set:** Civilian Containment, Faction Parity, & Meta-Achievements

## 🏗️ 1. NEW BUILDING TYPE: THE COMPLIANCE PEN
Instead of standard resource collectors, income is derived from **Population Density.**

### **The Regency: "Safe Harbor" Managed Communities**
 * **Aesthetic:** A windowless, brutalist shipping container with a low-res "Smiley Face" and a white picket fence decal.
 * **Logic:** Holds up to **10 Civilians.**
 * **Income:** Generates **$X/min** in "Administrative Fees."
 * **The "Bust":** If destroyed, 10 **Antagonistic Civilians** (Agitators) are released. ROE automatically jumps to Level 3.

### **The Sovereign Union: "Revolutionary Safehouses"**
 * **Aesthetic:** A generic storefront (Laundromat/Juice Bar) with a hidden cellar door.
 * **Logic:** Used to "Store" liberated civilians before they are moved off-map via **Tunnels.**
 * **Income:** One-time **"Gratitude Donation"** per head upon successful tunnel extraction.

### **The Oligarchy: "Labor Outsourcing Hubs"**
 * **Aesthetic:** A chain-link enclosure with a "Now Hiring" neon sign.
 * **Logic:** Civilians are converted into **"Contractors."**
 * **Income:** Passive income generated by proximity to resource nodes. If a civilian "Contractor" dies, the player receives a small **Insurance Payout.**

 * **Building_Pen.gd**: A new class inheriting from Building_Base. Needs a list[Civilian_ID] to track who is inside.
