Subject: University Quad Evolution

Phase 1: Pristine

Manicured green lawns rendered in a vibrant, digitized emerald pixel style.

Pristine white marble monuments and statues of "Founders" stand untouched.

Civilian "Student" units wander in predictable, circular pathing loops.

Visual clarity is high, with no smoke or debris obscuring the grid.

Phase 2: Occupied

Concrete "Stability Barriers" and coils of razor wire cut across the lawns.

Central fountain is drained and used as a temporary MRAP parking zone.

Large green-screen propaganda banners hang from the Student Library facade.

Flickering teal spotlights sweep the area; light surface scorching on pathways.

Phase 3: Shattered

Buildings reduced to skeletal frames with flickering "Signal Lost" neon signs.

Central quad is a mess of craters and smoldering Unmarked Van wrecks.

Heavy "static" and "glitch" particle effects overlaid on the green terminal palette.

Structural collapses create new chokepoints and block former transit pathways.
